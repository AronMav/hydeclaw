Replace `viewMode + liveMessages` dual-semantics with a `MessageSource` discriminated union, and make `streamGeneration` per-agent instead of module-scoped.

Purpose: Eliminate the root cause of history/live race conditions (Pitfall 5 from PITFALLS.md) and fix concurrent multi-agent stream killing (Pitfall 3). These two structural changes are prerequisites for reliable F5 restore in plan 02.

Output: `MessageSource` type in chat-store, all consumers migrated from `viewMode`, per-agent generation counter.
</objective>
